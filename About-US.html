About

Swastik Agro Industries

Founded in 1975, our firm is driven by a deep passion for agriculture and ploughing. With decades of experience, we deliver excellence at every stage of cultivation through high-standard mechanical and hydraulic ploughs manufactured from premium MS steel. Our products are engineered for durability, precision, and consistent superior performance, ensuring reliable results throughout the farming process.
